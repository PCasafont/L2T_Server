Adena
Adena is the main currency of this server. It is dropped by most monsters and most of the shop items are sold for adena.



Gold Einhasad
This is the small donations currency, with 100 of them you can get 1 Coins of Luck. Bosses drop them and if you can get some from events too.


Coin of Luck
The good old donations currency. They are your express pass to save some farming time.


Event - Apiga / Golden Apiga
These are special coins you can obtain during automated events and/or Gainak Siege. The better your points the more you'll get.
