Dimension Keeper:
How do you do? I'm a second-generation Spicula that was woken up by the Giant's voice, and I work to restore the Giants. My ability involves using runes to twist dimensions and join them for an instant. In other words, I can teleport others from one place to another instantly. With this ability, I make traveling more convenient for Spiculas who are working to recover the bodies of Giants.
For a small fee, I also offer my services to the  Awakened who can access high-level rune language. The fees I collect will be used to purchase enormous amount of gold required to restore the bodies of my masters. If you are Awakened, I recommend you to use my services to travel conveniently.
