Capture The Flag Event

Each team has a flag and the target of the event is to capture the enemy's flag and deliver it to yours.
How is it done? First you have to touch their flag. If you do, their flag will disappear and you will get a hero aura. Then, to complete the flag capture, you will have to return without dying and touch your team's flag.
To make a flag re-appear its wearer has to die or complete its capture.
The team which has captured more flags wins.
