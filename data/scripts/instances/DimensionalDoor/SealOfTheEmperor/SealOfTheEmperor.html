Seal Of The Emperor:

General Information:
Min/Max Players: 1/1
Type: PvE
Instance time: 60 Minutes
Reuse: 24 Hours
Difficulty: Easy
Status: Available
Description:
This Instance is the same as the last part of the retail Quest "Seal Of The Emperor" (Seven Signs). The zone is composed by many rooms where you will have different challenges, instance doors can be opened with the different instance items, at the end Anakim will request your help for defeat Lilith.


Reward Information: On this instance you will get Shiny Coin, the amount depends on the instance difficulty and instance minimum players.
These coins can be used at the Dimensional Door Shop.

Available Actions: Enter to the instance.
