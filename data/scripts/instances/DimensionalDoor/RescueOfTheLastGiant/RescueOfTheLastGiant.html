Rescue Of The Last Giant:

General Information:
Min/Max Players: 1/1
Type: PvE
Instance time: 60 Minutes
Reuse: 24 Hours
Difficulty: Easy
Status: Available
Description:
This Instance is the same as the retail Quest "Seize your destiny" (Awakening Quest). The zone is composed by 3 rooms where you will have different challenges, at the end you will have to fight with the Harnak's Wraith, which have the Last Giant imprisoned.


Reward Information: On this instance you will get Shiny Coin, the amount depends on the instance difficulty and instance minimum players.
These coins can be used at the Dimensional Door Shop.

Available Actions: Enter to the instance.
