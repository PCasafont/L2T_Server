Adena
You can get Adena practically at everywhere, can be used at any retail shop, at our Standard Shop (Magic Gem) or at the Auction System (Community Board).


Silver Shilen
You can find SS in almost every monster in the high level hunting zones. With it you can purchase more special items in the Luxury Shop. But most of these items are easier to be obtained by looting them directly from monsters!


Bloody Pa'agrio
The events currency! Depending on your performance in the events, you will be getting more or less of these items. With that currency you will be able to purchase unique appearances and some of the farmable items.


Event Apiga
The GM Events currency, can be used on the GM Events Shop!


Shiny Coin
That's the reward of the Dimensional Door instances. With a bunch of them you will have access to the Dimensional Bracelet, Zodiac Agathions, pets, special potions and several shirts and belts.


Blue Eva
When killing bosses, especially the ones in the World Altars, you will earn this kind of currency. With it you can buy very various time-limited talismans, protection potions, hats with +5 of all the base stats, special dyes and access to all the versions of the Talisman - Abundance.


Gold Einhasad
This is the small donations currency, with 100 of them you can get 1 Coins of Luck.


Coin of Luck
The good old donations currency, still tradeable. With them you can purchase Blessed Enchant Scrolls and very cool appearance items.
