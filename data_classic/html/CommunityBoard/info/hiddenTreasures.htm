Hidden Treasures

A group of 5 Treasure chests will appear at random locations from the map!
You can use the command .treasure to get different hints: Far Far Away: You are so far! Very Cold: Still far, but close to the location! Cold: It's at same location! Warm: Check in circles! Very Warm: Open your eyes, its near! Hot: It's arround you! Very Hot: It's really Close Extremely Hot: You're almost in front!
Go and find it before somebody else does!
